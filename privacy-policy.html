1Introduction

Piwi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Piwi mobile application (the "App").

By using Piwi, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

4Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data based on:

  • Contractual necessity: To provide the App's core features (account, race discovery, leaderboards)
  • Legitimate interests: Analytics, fraud prevention, and app improvement
  • Consent: For optional features like result claims with photo evidence

All third parties are contractually bound to use your data only for the purposes described above and to protect it in accordance with applicable laws.

6Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Password hashing: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms by Supabase Auth.
  • Row-level security: Database access is restricted so users can only access their own data.
  • Image compression: Evidence photos are resized to 1200px width and compressed to 80% quality before storage.
  • No plaintext storage: Sensitive data is never stored in plain text.

7Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the App's services.

Data Type Retention Period
Account and profile data Until account deletion
Race results and claims Until account deletion (or as required by race organizers)
Evidence photos Until the associated result claim is deleted
Analytics data 12 months (PostHog default retention)
Deleted accounts 30 days after soft deletion, then permanently erased

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time:
1. Go to Settings → Delete Account
2. Confirm the deletion
3. Your account will be soft-deleted immediately and permanently erased within 30 days

When you delete your account:

  • Your profile and personal data are marked for deletion
  • Your result claims and evidence photos are removed
  • Your auth credentials are invalidated
  • Your wishlist and follow relationships are deleted

Note: Anonymized race results that have been published to public leaderboards may be retained in aggregated form.

9Children's Privacy

Piwi is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us at piwi.support@gmail.com and we will delete the information promptly.

10International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed in the region where your Supabase project is hosted. Analytics data is processed in the European Union (via PostHog's EU infrastructure).

If you are located outside these regions, your data may be transferred to and processed in these locations. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
